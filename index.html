Robots and Science fiction, two things that have been intertwined as science fiction has increased in its popularity throughout the past century. But, as technology and the integration of robots has increased the way we portray robots in science fiction has changed as well. We take a deep dive into the change of the approach that writers take to showcase robots in science fiction. To tell this story, we brought in different guests and even have a special interview with an archivist.
